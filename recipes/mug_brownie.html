Mug Brownie

Description

This is a delicious little treat! This mini mug brownie is easy to make and perfectly portioned.

Pro tip: serve with a scoop of ice cream on top.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbl flour
  • 5 tsp sugar
  • 1 tbl cocoa powder
  • 1 tbl vegetable oil
  • 1 tbl water
  • dash of salt

Steps

  1. Mix dry ingredients together in mug.
  2. Mix in the wet ingredients.
  3. Microwave on high for about 40 seconds, brownie should still be wet in the center.
