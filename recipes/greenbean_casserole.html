Green Bean Casserole

Description

Classic green bean casserole to serve a crowd. Makes about 18 servings.

Ingredients

  • 3 cans (10 1/2 oz each) condensed cream of mushroom soup
  • 2 1/4 cups milk
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper (or to taste)
  • 6 cans (14 1/2 ox each) green beans, drained
  • 4 cups crispy fried onions

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 350ÂºF.
  2. In a large baking dish, mix soup, milk, and pepper until smooth.
  3. Stir in green beans and 2 cups of the crispy fried onions.
  4. Bake for 30 minutes or until heated through.
  5. Stir the casserole, then top with the remaining 2 cups of onions.
  6. Bake 5 minutes more or until onions are gold and crisp.
